# Authentication Feature

Authentication manages everything related to securely logging in users, maintaining their sessions, and authorizing access across the platform.

## Why in the dictionary?
Authentication is the backbone of any personalized application. We rely on JWT tokens to keep APIs stateless and fast.

## What is it?
- A standard email/password login flow.
- Support for Single Sign-On (SSO) with Google.
